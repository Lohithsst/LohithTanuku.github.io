5.

Service

2023

Student-Initiated Project (Vice-Chairperson)

I started a student-initiated project with some of my classmates. We provided 3D printing lessons to 34 primary 5 students from Yew Tee Primary School. We aimed to foster their creativity and cultivate a passion for STEM subjects. Over three days, we introduced the students to various types of 3D printers and guided them in using TinkerCAD, a 3D modelling software. Through this software, they could design their unique creations. We organised a competition to assess their newly acquired skills, allowing the students to showcase their talents and abilities. We aimed to empower the students through hands-on learning and encourage their enthusiasm for STEM.

2023

Python in Five (Class-initiated project)

Python in Five was a student-initiated project that conducted a 5-hour beginners' Python programming workshop for 10-13-year-olds. The workshop consisted of five sessions, each lasting 5 hours, and took place in March 2023. With an impressive turnout, we had nearly 70 enthusiastic participants. Our primary objective was to ignite the participants' curiosity in programming, recognizing its growing importance in the 21st century.
