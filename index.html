Projects

2022-2023

Kichn

Kichn is a kitchen inventory management app designed to reduce food waste. It helps users track their FairPrice products and expiry dates. Built with a Python backend and web frontend (using WindiCSS, AlpineJS, and HTMX), the app includes inventory management, a grocery list, and real-time sharing. Kichn's barcode scanner and instant search make it quick and easy to find products. The app uses Redis for its database, AIOHTTP for the web server, Jinja as the HTML templating engine, and Meilisearch for instant search. Kichn scrapes data from the FairPrice API to ensure an extensive and accurate product database. By using Kichn, users can save money, reduce waste, and enhance their shopping experience.

2021

EasyPlant

EZPlant is the essential plant-growing companion app that caters to every need of plant owners. Its plant-identification quiz helps users discover the ideal plant to meet their specific needs. EZPlant offers customised gardening tips for each user's plant type, including reminders for watering their plants. The app also features a marketplace that allows users to discover and explore shops that sell plants. EZPlant is the must-have app for gardening enthusiasts, whether they are beginners or experienced gardeners. With its intuitive user interface and comprehensive features, EZPlant is the perfect solution for taking gardening skills to the next level.

Service Learning Projects

2023

Student-Initiated Project (Vice-Chairperson)

I started a student-initiated project with some of my classmates. We provided 3D printing lessons to 34 primary 5 students from Yew Tee Primary School. We aimed to foster their creativity and cultivate a passion for STEM subjects. Over three days, we introduced the students to various types of 3D printers. We guided them in using TinkerCAD, a 3D modelling software. Through this software, they could design their unique creations. We organised a competition to assess their newly acquired skills, allowing the students to showcase their talents and abilities. We aimed to empower the students through hands-on learning and encourage their enthusiasm for STEM.
